Johnny's Spaghetti

Backstory & Creator

This recipe has been in my family for generations. I first learned it from my grandmother, who taught me the importance of fresh ingredients and love in every dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Cook spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
  3. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil for 1 minute.
  4. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Toss the spaghetti in the sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with fresh basil on top.
